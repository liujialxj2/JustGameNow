How to Play

1

Control Direction

Use the left and right arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to rotate the hexagon and position the incoming blocks.

2

Match Colors

Align three or more blocks of the same color to clear them from the playing field and earn points.

3

Prevent Overflow

Keep blocks from reaching the outer edge of the hexagon. If blocks overflow, the game ends.

4

Achieve High Score

The longer you play and the more blocks you clear, the higher your score will be. Compete for the top spot!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hextris Challenge free to play?

Yes, Hextris Challenge is completely free to play in your browser, with no downloads or installations required. Just visit our website and start playing immediately!

Can I play Hextris on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Hextris Challenge is fully optimized for mobile play with touch controls. Simply visit our website on your mobile browser to enjoy the game on the go.

How do I save my high score?

Your high score is automatically saved to your local device. For global leaderboard participation, you can create a free account which will track your scores across all your devices.

Are there different difficulty levels?

Hextris Challenge features a progressive difficulty system. As you play and your score increases, the game gradually becomes more challenging with faster blocks and more complex patterns.

Can I pause the game?

Yes, you can pause the game at any time by pressing the spacebar on desktop or tapping the pause icon on mobile devices. This allows you to take a break without losing your progress.
